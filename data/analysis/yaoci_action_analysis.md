### 2.2 Position Characteristics

**初爻 (First Line):**
- Most common: 用 (use), 往 (go)
- Tendency: Cautious actions, foundational movements
- Typical outcomes: 無咎 (no blame), 吉 (auspicious) when restrained

**二爻 (Second Line):**
- Most common: 得 (gain), 征 (expedition)
- Tendency: Moderate, centered actions
- Typical outcomes: 貞吉 (correct and auspicious)

**三爻 (Third Line):**
- Most common: 往 (go), 見 (see)
- Tendency: Risky, transitional position
- Typical outcomes: 吝 (regret), 厲 (danger), 凶 (misfortune)

**四爻 (Fourth Line):**
- Most common: 遇 (meet), 得 (gain), 用 (use)
- Tendency: Meeting opportunities, careful advancement
- Typical outcomes: 無咎 (no blame), conditional success

**五爻 (Fifth Line):**
- Most common: 用 (use), 得 (gain)
- Tendency: Leadership actions, proper use of authority
- Typical outcomes: 吉 (auspicious), 無不利 (nothing unfavorable)

**上爻 (Top Line):**
- Most common: 征 (expedition), 失/喪 (lose)
- Tendency: Extremes, endings
- Typical outcomes: 凶 (misfortune) or 吉 (auspicious) - polarized

## 4. Pattern Analysis by Yao Position

### 4.1 初爻 Patterns

**Positive Actions:**
- 復自道 → 吉
- 素履往 → 無咎
- 无妄往 → 吉
- 允升 → 大吉

**Negative Actions:**
- 壯于趾，征凶
- 以往吝

## 6. Key Insights

### 6.1 Position-Action Wisdom

1. **初爻**: "When beginning, be humble and restrained. Aggressive starts fail."
2. **二爻**: "Hold the center. Balanced action succeeds; extreme caution or boldness fails."
3. **三爻**: "The dangerous middle. Expect difficulty; maintain integrity through hardship."
4. **四爻**: "Seek connections. Meeting others and adapting brings success."
5. **五爻**: "Lead with virtue. Proper authority succeeds; rigid control fails."
6. **上爻**: "Know when to retreat. Advance at the end brings misfortune."

### 6.2 Action Timing Principles

1. **往 (Going)**: Best at 初 with preparation, worst at 三 and 上
2. **征 (Campaign)**: Best at 二 with center/legitimacy, worst at 上 (extreme)
3. **復 (Return)**: Best early (初, 二), essential at 上 to avoid disaster
4. **涉 (Crossing)**: Best when conditions (有孚, 利貞) are met
5. **見 (Seeing)**: Generally positive; seeing "大人" always beneficial

### 6.3 Contextual Modifiers

**Positive Modifiers:**
- 有孚 (sincerity) → improves outcomes
- 貞 (correctness) → stabilizes outcomes
- 中行 (middle way) → reduces risk
- 利 (beneficial) → indicates good timing

**Negative Modifiers:**
- 匪正 (incorrect) → worsens outcomes
- 極/窮 (extreme) → leads to reversal
- 勿用 (don't use) → timing is wrong
